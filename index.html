The Silicon Slopes Advantage

Utah offers a concentrated, cost-effective, and culturally aligned talent pool with deep SaaS experience. The ecosystem is dense, interconnected, and fueled by major tech anchors and strong universities.

Utah Tech Talent Concentration

The majority of Utah's tech jobs are concentrated in the Salt Lake and Provo-Orem metro areas.

Local Tech Stack Alignment

Utah's talent pool shows strong alignment with Podium's core technologies, including a surprisingly vibrant Elixir community.

The National Talent Ocean

The U.S. remote market provides unparalleled access to a vast, diverse, and senior-heavy talent pool, especially for niche skills like Elixir and advanced AI/ML, but at a premium cost.

Seniority Bias in Remote Roles

Senior-level jobs are significantly more likely to be offered as remote positions, creating a talent pool rich in experience.

Access to Niche Expertise

The national pool offers orders of magnitude more depth for highly specialized Elixir and AI/ML talent.

Podium's Path Forward: A Bifurcated Sourcing Strategy

The optimal strategy is a two-pronged approach that leverages the unique strengths of both the local and remote talent markets, balancing cost-effectiveness with access to elite, niche talent.

For Core Python/React Roles
